MEET LACEY

Lacey Williams is a veteran songwriter, performer and musician. She has played on a professional level for a number of artists, contributing keys, fiddle and background harmonies. Lacey has also headlined various stages as the lead singer of the duet Drew & Lacey, where she sings and plays with her husband. Lacey has also established herself as songwriter in Nashville, writing songs for Drew & Lacey as well as other Nashville country artists.

Drew & Lacey are a Nashville-based, husband and wife duo. Their folk and bluegrass backgrounds have combined with a Nashville country vibe to create a unique sound, full of rich harmonies and stellar musicianship. Their recently recorded EP, produced by Jason Deere, is available on iTunes. The album demonstrates their vocal, instrumental and songwriting talent. The two instrumentalists together play keys, fiddle, mandolin and guitar and provide an entertaining and energetic stage show. It's this energy and musicianship that has allowed them to share the stage with country superstars, LeAnn Rimes, JoDee Messina, Due West, as well as Ryan Shupe & the Rubberband.
