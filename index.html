Drew & Lacey

Monochrome

Drew & Lacey are a Nashville-based, husband and wife duo.
Their folk and bluegrass backgrounds have combined with a Nashville country vibe to create a unique sound, full of rich harmonies and stellar musicianship. Their second EP, titled "Monochrome," fuses pop, country, and folk elements. The album, produced by Brandon Metcalf, features original songwriting and showcases the collaboration of the duo with musicians from multiple genres. The album demonstrates their vocal, instrumental and songwriting talent. The two instrumentalists together play keys, fiddle, mandolin and guitar and provide an entertaining and energetic stage show. Monochrome, as well as their first self-titled EP, are available on iTunes.
